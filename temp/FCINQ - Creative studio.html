FCINQ [ɛfsɛ̃k] n. 1. The key that refreshes the screen.
2. What originally started out as a team of graphic designers, animators and developers is today a team of thirty people whose roles range from strategy developers to brand content and UX Design. Whatever the format may be, their focus is to provide the best quality service for creating experiences and brand worlds that convey emotion, feeling and that make communication a successful exchange with the final user.
3. Our projects say more about us than long speeches do.
