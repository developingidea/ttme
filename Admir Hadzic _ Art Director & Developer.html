Chasing Perfection

I think for most creatives and designers one of the biggest challenges in their profession is to create a personal portfolio. Somehow, regardless of how much you try, you end up in a cicrle of chasing for perfection and possible frustration.

Exploration through exploration, you somehow feel its never good enough. Your keep striving for more, walking the thin line of expression and aesthetic beauty. Personally, its one of my favourite experiences. It allows me to look back and over again explore and examine my current artistic direction and thinking.

Focus

I really tried to focus hard on expressing the maturity of my current work, artistic phase, mindset, approach, aspirations and rules that guide me through my professional and personal life.

Less is more

Word taken, the final product delivers perfectly aligned content with little to no room or need for additional elements and information. For this challenge, in particular, I decided to focus on delivering what's expected and needed in terms of visuals, interactions and content, rather than personal preferences.

There is a minimal set of interactions, accompanied by animations and guestures followed by simplistic and easy to handle static content.
